## Bracket Library

Audience-specific vocabulary to plug into formulas. Build these for each audience:

| Bracket | Description | Examples |
|---------|-------------|----------|
| **(OUTCOME)** | What they really want | "book more sales calls", "lose 10kg", "grow to 10k followers" |
| **(PROBLEM)** | What's in the way | "no time", "inconsistent leads", "algorithm changes" |
| **(MISTAKE)** | Predictable error they make | "posting without a hook", "skipping the CTA" |
| **(BAD HABIT)** | Behavior keeping them stuck | "doom scrolling", "perfectionism", "shiny object syndrome" |
| **(MYTH)** | Belief that needs debunking | "you need expensive gear", "post at 3pm for best reach" |
| **(COMMON ADVICE)** | Cliché advice to challenge | "be consistent", "just provide value" |
| **(HACK)** | Small surprising tactic | "blur the key ingredient", "use numbers in hooks" |
| **(METRIC)** | Proof number | "watch time", "save rate", "close rate" |
| **(TIMEFRAME)** | Time constraint | "7 days", "30 seconds", "before your next post" |
| **(NUMBER)** | Specific quantity | "3 steps", "$10k/month", "100,000 views" |
| **(TRENDY TACTIC)** | Current fad in the niche | "AI agents", "faceless reels", "carousel posts" |
| **(MILESTONE)** | Meaningful checkpoint | "first 1k followers", "first client", "10k months" |
| **(COUNTERINTUITIVE ACTION)** | Unexpected move | "post less often", "ignore the algorithm" |

## Formula Templates by Angle

### Curiosity Formulas
- "Why is no one talking about (TOPIC) this way?"
- "What actually happens when you drop (BAD HABIT) for (TIMEFRAME)?"
- "Is (MYTH) the real reason you're stuck at (MILESTONE)?"
- "Do you really need (RESOURCE) to reach (OUTCOME)?"
- "What's the hidden cost of ignoring (PROBLEM) for (TIMEFRAME)?"

### Bold Callout Formulas
- "(NUMBER) reasons your (STRATEGY) isn't working—and #(NUMBER) hurts"
- "You're not lazy—you're stuck in (BAD HABIT)"
- "You'll never hit (OUTCOME) while you're still doing (TRENDY TACTIC)"
- "(MYTH) is the slowest possible way to get (OUTCOME)"
- "Let's be honest... (COMMON ADVICE) is why you're still at (MILESTONE)"

### Cliffhanger Formulas
- "Give me (TIMEFRAME) and I'll rewire how you see (TOPIC)"
- "By step (NUMBER), (OUTCOME) will start to feel automatic"
- "Stay to the end—the (HACK) I give you there is the game-changer"
- "If you're stuck at (MILESTONE), the third mistake will sting"

### Fix-It Formulas
- "Here's how to hit (OUTCOME) in (NUMBER) steps"
- "If (PROBLEM) keeps coming back, try this instead: (COUNTERINTUITIVE ACTION)"
- "Low (METRIC)? Steal this two-minute ritual"
- "Want faster (OUTCOME)? Stop doing (ACTION) and do this instead"

### Mirror Formulas
- "You're doing (TRENDY TACTIC), but it's not moving your (METRIC)"
- "You say you want (OUTCOME), but your calendar screams (BAD HABIT)"
- "You've hit (OUTCOME) before—what's actually stopping you now?"
- "Every push toward (MILESTONE) starts strong... then dies in the same place"

### Challenge Formulas
- "Bet you didn't know (MYTH) is what's holding you back"
- "Prove me wrong: use this (HACK) for (TIMEFRAME)"
- "I bet your 'quick fix' is why (PROBLEM) keeps coming back"
- "You're one tweak away from (OUTCOME)—and it's not what you think"

## Visual Hook Principles

For video hooks, the visual is as important as the text:

**Clean first frame:**
- Subject clear, background simple
- Text in "safe zone" (not covered by platform UI)
- Text smaller than you think
- Good lighting creates depth

**Deepen the mystery visually:**
- Blur key ingredients/reveals
- Create visual contrast (fit person + bowl of carbs)
- Don't let them solve the puzzle from the thumbnail

**Text overlay best practices:**
- Use font weight hierarchy (bold key words)
- Match a color from the image
- Subtle outer glow for readability

## Quick Quality Check

Before finalizing any hook:

- [ ] **Specific?** Clear topic/outcome/problem mentioned?
- [ ] **Emotional?** Touches frustration, desire, curiosity, or ego?
- [ ] **Simple?** Would a 12-year-old understand on first read?
- [ ] **Tension?** Conflict present (myth vs truth, old vs new)?
- [ ] **Pattern Interrupt?** Stands out in a feed of generic advice?
- [ ] **Loop open?** Question planted that requires watching to answer?
