# ArqOne Marketing — Department Prompt

> Central marketing intelligence for the ArqOne Labs portfolio.

---

## Purpose

This folder holds all cross-portfolio marketing assets, brand guidelines, campaign materials, and shared content strategy. Individual platform marketing lives in each platform's `Marketing/` subfolder — this folder is for ArqOne Labs-level brand work.

---

## What Lives Here

### Brand Assets (`./`)
- `ArqOne_Labs_Logo.png` — Full logo (mark + wordmark, square format, dark background)
- `ArqOne_Labs_Logo_Horizontal.png` — Horizontal lockup (mark + wordmark side-by-side)
- `design_philosophy.md` — The "Precision Flux" design philosophy behind the brand identity

### Brand Guidelines
- Primary color: Electric Teal `#00DCD0` / `rgb(0, 220, 205)`
- Background: Near-black `#08080C` / `rgb(8, 8, 12)`
- Accent glow: Teal at reduced opacity for depth effects
- Typography: Jura (headings/wordmark), Geist Mono (technical/labels)
- Logo mark: Geometric "A" triangle with integrated vertical "One" bar and glowing vertex nodes

### Naming Rules
- **ArqOne** — always one word, capital A and capital O. Never: Arqone, ARQONE, arq-one, Arq One
- **ArqOne Labs** — the full entity name, used on websites, legal, investor materials
- **Platform names** — each platform keeps its own brand identity (BrandSprk, ShieldPilot, SalesX, Plymio, AI Navigator)
- **"A product of ArqOne Labs"** — standard footer attribution on platform websites

## Voice Guidelines

### ArqOne Labs (parent brand)
- Confident, direct, builder energy
- Speaks to the portfolio thesis: AI that replaces the need for specialist hires
- Used in: LinkedIn, investor decks, partnership conversations, arqonelabs.com

### Platform Brands
Each platform has its own voice (defined in its own CLAUDE.md). The parent brand voice does NOT override platform voices — it exists alongside them for portfolio-level communications.

## Content Strategy

- **AI Navigator** is the credibility engine — its content (book, course, briefs) positions Piero as a thought leader, which creates trust for all other platforms
- **Cross-sell opportunities**: ShieldPilot → SalesX (same SME buyer), AI Navigator → all platforms (education → product adoption)
- **ArqOne Labs LinkedIn** should showcase the portfolio thesis, not individual product features
